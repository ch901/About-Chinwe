ABOUT MYSELF

My name is Chinwe Amarachukwu OKpala. Born to the family of Rev. and Mrs Osita Okpala. i am Igbo by tribe, but was born and raised in the northern state of Gombe. i am the third of seven children. i am about 5"8 tall, dark in complexion with a gaptooth and facial mark.

i started my academic journey by attending F.C.E.T. Staff school Gombe wher i had my Part of my Primary education, i had cause to live with my gand parents in the Village where i completed my Primary School at Eke-Achina Primary School, Achina. i came back to Gombe, and enrolled in Gombe High School, Gombe where i wrote my WAEC and did excellently and proceeded to University of Jos to read Law. After five years at the University of Jos, i graduated and was awarded a Bachelor of Law Degree which qualified me to enrol into the Nigerian Law School. i was called to the Nigerian bar. After My NYSC in Lagos, i moved to Abuja where I had a burgeoning legal Practice under the name and style "Clearharbour Legal Consult". in 2017 i applied to University of where I got a post Graduate Diploma in Education(PGDE) and in 2019 i got admission the Obafemi Awolowo University to study Business Administration and a Masters in Business Administration (MBA).

By the time I got back from Ife in 2021, so much had happenned and it seemed the world has changed drastically. the devastating effect of Covid19 Pandemic, made most businesses to move online and that led to fall in demand for cetain services. in order to remain relevant i sought a change in career by enrolling in to Stutern to become a software engineer.
